title: Update Apps
keywords: [Sailos, update application, app settings, cloud platform, deploy app]
description: Update your application settings on Sailos easily. Click the "Update" button on the app details page to make changes anytime.
---

After deploying an application on [Sailos](https://usw.sailos.io), you can update the application settings at any
time. Just click the "Update" button at the top-right of the app details page. When you're done, click on the "Update"
button at the top-right.
